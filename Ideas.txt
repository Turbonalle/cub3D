Jailbreaker:

- Get to the goal of the map without getting seen by the guards.
- Throw an egg (or something) to distract them.
- If you walk into a guard's FOV, they'll start chasing you.


Puzzle:

- Some wall blocks are movable.
- Find your way to the goal by moving some blocks.
- For increased difficulty, you need to find keys or press buttons in order to unlock doors.


Lava Sppedrun:

- Find your way to the end before the lava catches you.
- The lava could be shown as a wall of lava.
- The lava would use an algorithm that slowly fills empty blocks on the map.
